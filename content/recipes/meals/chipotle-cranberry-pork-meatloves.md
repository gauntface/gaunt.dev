## Ingredients

- 2 Sweet potatoes
- 1 Shallot
- 8 oz Brussel sprouts
- 1 tsp Garlic powder
- 1 tsp Chipotle powder
- 1 tbsp Flour
- 2 tbsp Cranberry jam
- 1/4 cup Panko
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1/2 Chicken stock
- 10 oz Ground pork

- (Optional) 1 1/2 tbsp Sour cream

This recipe is for 2 servings

## Directions

- Turn on overn to 425 degrees (Roast)
- Start boiling salted water for the sweet potatoes

### Prep

- Peel and dice sweet potatoes, cutting them into 1/2" pieces
- Halve and peel the shallot
  - Grate 1/2 shallot on the large holes of a box grater
  - Mince the other 1/2
- Trim and 1/2 the brussel sprouts
- [Glaze] In a small bowl combine until smooth:
  - Cranberry jam
  - Half chipotle powder
  - A tbhsp of ketchup
  - Pinch of salt


### Cook

- Add sweet potatoes to the boiling water
  - Cook them for 15 minutes
  - Save a 1/4 cup of the cooking liquid
  - Drain and cover in pot (mash later)
- In a large bowl combine:
  - Grated shallot
  - Ground pork
  - Garlic powder
  - Panko
  - A tbsp of ketchup
  - Salt & pepper
- Form the mixture into two loaves
- Oil a baking sheet
- Put the loaves of one side of a baking sheet
- Put brussel sprouts on the other side of the baking sheet
  - Oil the burssel sprouts
  - Sprinkle with salt & pepper
- Roast sprouts and loaves for 15 mins
- Melt 1 tbsp of butter in a frying pan (medium heat)
- Add minced shallot and  cook stirring for 3-4 mins
- Sprinkle with flour, stir to combine
- Whisk mustard & stock concentrate
- Bring to simmer and cook, stirring occassionaly until thickened, 1-3 minutes
- After loaves have cooked, brush with the cranberry glaze
  - Return to the oven for another 5-6 minutes
- Mash the sweet potatoes:
  - Add 1 tbsp of butter
  - Add a pinch of chipotle powder
  - (Optional) Add sour cream
  - Add splashes of cooking liquid as needed
- Slice meat loaves
- Serve:
  - Slice meat loave
  - Brussel sprouts
  - Gravy over the loaves
