Pin-It is a simple Chrome extension that makes it easy to keep a set of tabs pinned.

Why?

Do you like having e-mail, slack, calendar, and other sites open while you work?

If so, this extension is for you! Never forget to open a website or forget where the tab went.

Configure Pin-It with the sites you want it to open, and every time you click on the extension icon, it’ll set your pinned tabs to your sites.

How?

Step 1

Download the Chrome extension from the Chrome Webstore here.

Step 2

Open the options page by clicking on the Pin-It extension icon.

Step 3

Click the Edit URLs button

Step 4

Add your sites and click the Save button

Step 5

Now, click the Pin-It extension icon to update your pinned tabs

Step 6 - Optional

You can make the Pin-It extension stick to your URL bar by pinning the extension itself.

Once it’s pinned, you can click on the Pin-It icon to set up your tabs.

Changing your URLs

You can change the pinned URLs at any time by right-clicking the Pin-It extension (or the menu icon) and selecting Options.
